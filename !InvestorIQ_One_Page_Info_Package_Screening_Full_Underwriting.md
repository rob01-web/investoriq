# InvestorIQ One-Page Info Package

## Document-Driven Real Estate Intelligence for Smarter Property Decisions

**InvestorIQ** transforms uploaded property documents into professional real estate reports for investors, brokers, lenders, and operators. Instead of forcing users to manually rebuild every deal in spreadsheets or calculators, InvestorIQ extracts, validates, organizes, and presents what the source documents support.

**Core promise:** InvestorIQ does not invent missing deal facts. It uses what the uploaded documents support, clearly identifies source limitations, and avoids unsupported investment or financing claims.

---

## Product Ladder

### 1. InvestorIQ Screening Report

**Purpose:** Fast first-pass deal triage.

The Screening Report helps investors quickly decide whether a property is worth deeper diligence. It is designed for investors reviewing multiple opportunities and needing a clean, document-backed way to narrow the list.

**Primary inputs:** T12 / operating statement and rent roll.

**Key outputs:**

```text
Property operating snapshot
T12 income / expense / NOI summary
Rent roll occupancy and rent-positioning summary
Annual in-place rent
Annual market rent
Annual rent upside
Rent gap percentage
Expense ratio
NOI margin
Break-even occupancy
Source coverage and document reliability notes
```

**Best for:** Investors screening multiple deals and deciding which properties deserve deeper diligence.

## Report Philosophy

InvestorIQ is designed around trust.

```text
Bad core documents cannot generate a report.
Valid documents should produce a useful report.
Unsupported sections should be omitted, qualified, disclosed, or marked not assessed.
Optional documents should not create fake certainty.
InvestorIQ does not use BUY / SELL / HOLD recommendations.
InvestorIQ does not promise loan approval or lender commitment.
```

InvestorIQ’s advantage is document discipline: it shows what was used, what was not used, what was context-only, and what remains missing for deeper diligence.
